        Stream Warrior

        Log in.

        Or don't. You won't able to see streams that you follow with your Twitch account, but that's up to you.

        Pick a stream.

        You can either pick one from a preset event, your "following" list, or type in the broadcaster's name. Check out the top right of the page for these options.

        Watch endlessly.

        Become the ultimate stream warrior...? Go watch every stream until your computer explodes.

        Tips

        • Want to chat with one stream, but watch another? Just use the "Change Chat" option and scroll to the other stream.
        • The "Following" list can only load a maximum of 25 streams. Twitch's rules, not mine!
        • The number of streams that you can add is up to you, but be mindful of your computer's stress.
        • You can log out of the app by hovering over your name and clicking "Log Out".
